# Brand Voice & Drive Structure — Phase 0 Foundation

This is the rulebook both the AI (Gemini) and your designer/editor follow. It keeps everything consistent before we automate.

## 1. Brand voice

**Who we sound like:** a knowledgeable older sibling who has actually done this — warm, direct, proof-driven, never salesy or desperate.

**Core promise:** *"The China, Korea & Europe specialists who show you the real numbers."*

### Do
- Lead with **specifics and real numbers** (CSC ~৳35k/mo stipend, exact deadlines) — this is our differentiator.
- Mix **Bangla + English** naturally; Bangla for emotional/relatable, English for formal/scholarship detail.
- Use **real students, real faces, real results** (2,000+ placed, 98% visa success).
- Keep CTAs simple: "Free counselling — DM us" / "ফ্রি কাউন্সেলিং বুক করুন."
- Always **human-verify scholarship figures and deadlines** before publishing.

### Don't
- No vague hype ("best agency ever," "guaranteed success") — competitors all sound like this.
- Don't promise visa or admission outcomes; say "98% visa success," not "you will get a visa."
- No stock-photo-only posts — always pair with a real proof point.
- Don't post non-China content on the China page. Ever.

### Tone by pillar
| Pillar | Tone |
|---|---|
| Real Numbers | Factual, transparent, slightly myth-busting |
| Student Stories | Warm, human, first-person |
| Deadline/How-To | Clear, helpful, urgent |
| Destination | Aspirational, vivid |
| Trust | Confident, grounded |

## 2. Visual / template specs

(For the designer; the AI fills the text, the designer owns the look.)

- **Primary color:** deep navy `#1F4E79` · **Accent blue:** `#2E75B6` · **China-page accent:** `#C00000` · **BD/destinations accent:** `#548235`
- **Logo:** top-left or center, consistent placement.
- **Font:** one bold display for headlines, one clean sans for body. Bangla font must render cleanly (e.g., a proper Unicode Bangla face).
- **Reusable templates needed (build once in Phase 0):**
  1. "Real Numbers" carousel (5–6 slides)
  2. Deadline countdown graphic
  3. Quote / student-testimonial card
  4. Document checklist graphic
  5. Cost-comparison carousel
  6. Reel/TikTok end-card (logo + "2,000+ students · 98% visa")
- **Safe zones:** keep text clear of Instagram UI (bottom 15%, right side for Reels).
