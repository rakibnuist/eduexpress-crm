# Content Engine — EduExpress International

**Channels:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok (Bangla + English)
**Cost:** Zero — Gemini Flash for drafting, Meta Graph API + TikTok API for posting, in-house designer for graphics.
**Built on:** the five gaps from the competitor analysis.

---

## 1. Five content pillars

Each pillar maps to a gap rivals are ignoring. Aim for this weekly mix.

| # | Pillar | What it does | Weekly share |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Real Numbers** | Actual costs, stipends, scholarship amounts (CSC ~৳35k/mo, Hungary, GKS). The lane nobody owns. | 30% |
| 2 | **Student Stories** | Real faces from the 2,000+ — "where are they now," visa-approval moments, arrival videos. | 25% |
| 3 | **Deadline & How-To** | Application steps, document checklists, deadline countdowns (CSC ~30 Apr, GKS, Stipendium Hungaricum). | 20% |
| 4 | **Destination Spotlight** | China / Korea / UK / Hungary life, campuses, cities, weather, food, part-time work. | 15% |
| 5 | **Trust & Behind-the-Scenes** | The team, office, named consultants answering FAQs in Bangla. | 10% |

---

## 2. Channel playbook

**Facebook** — primary lead engine. Longer Bangla posts, carousels, link posts to CRM lead form. Best for parents + serious applicants.

**Instagram** — visual proof. Reels of student stories, carousel "real numbers" infographics, Stories for deadline countdowns. Reach younger applicants.

**TikTok** — *the open field.* 30–60s Bangla videos: a consultant on camera answering one question ("কত টাকা লাগে চীনে পড়তে?"). Low production, high trust. Rivals are absent here — move fast.

**Cross-posting rule:** every Reel is also a TikTok; every infographic carousel posts to both FB and IG. One asset, three placements.

---

## 3. Cadence

- **Facebook:** 1 post/day (7/week)
- **Instagram:** 5 feed posts + daily Stories
- **TikTok:** 3–4 videos/week
- **Best Bangladesh posting times:** 1–3 PM and 8–10:30 PM (after work/dinner). Schedule via n8n + Meta Graph API.

## 6. Production workflow (zero-cost, n8n-orchestrated)
1. **Ideate** — this calendar feeds the queue; Gemini Flash drafts caption variations from each hook.
2. **Design** — in-house designer builds carousels/graphics from a reusable brand template; student videos shot on phone.
3. **Approve** — Rakib reviews a daily/weekly batch (keep a simple approval step).
4. **Schedule & post** — n8n pushes to FB/IG via Meta Graph API and to TikTok via TikTok API at the peak windows.
5. **Measure** — pull reach/engagement weekly; double down on the pillar that converts to counselling bookings.
