# Competitor Analysis — Bangladesh Study-Abroad Market

**Prepared for:** EduExpress International (Dhanmondi, Dhaka)
**Date:** 13 June 2026
**Purpose:** Map the competitive landscape, find messaging gaps, and define the angles EduExpress can own.

---

## 1. The competitive set

The Bangladesh study-abroad market splits into three tiers. EduExpress competes mainly in Tier 2 and should defend against Tier 1 on trust, not budget.

**Tier 1 — Global networks (high ad spend, broad destinations)**
- **IDP Bangladesh** — 50+ years of brand equity, IELTS owner, heavy on UK/Australia/Canada. Free counselling as the hook.
- **AECC Global**, **PFEC Global**, **KC Overseas (studies-overseas)** — multinational agencies, strong process, generic "study abroad" positioning.

**Tier 2 — Established local consultancies (EduExpress's direct rivals)**
- **Sangen Edu** — 18+ years, 300+ universities, UK/China/US/Canada. Longevity is their main claim.
- **GSC Global Solutions** — "top 5 in Bangladesh," Europe + UK + China focus.
- **Edumax** — "500+ students," visa-guidance angle, China/UK.
- **Roushan Educare** — 32 countries, personalized service; runs dedicated South Korea landing pages.
- **GoGlobal**, **Adaptive Consultant** — Korea/China/UK/USA, "free assistance" hooks.

**Tier 3 — Niche destination specialists**
- **GK Consultancy** — positions as "Bangladesh's leading South Korea consultancy," claims 99% visa success, GKS scholarship guidance.
- **FICC**, **SCC**, **Sagor Consultancy**, **IGC** — China specialists built around the CSC / Chinese Government Scholarship application.

## 2. How EduExpress stacks up

| Proof point | EduExpress | Typical Tier-2 rival | Edge? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Years operating | 8+ | 15–18+ (Sangen) | Rivals win on age |
| Students enrolled | 2,000+ | 500+ (Edumax) | **EduExpress wins** |
| University partners | 100+ | 300+ (Sangen) | Rivals claim more |
| Visa success rate | 98% | 99% (GK) | Parity |
| Destination focus | China, Korea, UK, Hungary | Scattered / broad | **EduExpress is more focused** |

**Read:** EduExpress can't out-shout Tier 1 on spend or out-age Sangen. But the **2,000+ enrolled** number is stronger than most Tier-2 rivals' claims, and the **China + Korea + Hungary** focus is sharper than the "we do 32 countries" crowd. Lead with outcomes and specialization, not breadth.

---

## 3. What everyone is saying (the sea of sameness)

Almost every competitor leans on the same four claims:
1. "Free counselling / free assistance"
2. "X% visa success rate"
3. "Partnered with N universities"
4. "Expert guidance from application to visa"

Because everyone says these, **none of them differentiate.** They are table stakes, not hooks.

## 4. Where the gaps are — angles EduExpress can own

These are under-served by competitors and match EduExpress's real strengths and zero-cost content stack:

1. **Radical transparency on cost & scholarships.** Rivals say "scholarships available." Almost none publish real, current numbers — CSC stipend (~৳35k/month), tuition waivers, what a Korea/Hungary year actually costs. **Own the "real numbers" content lane.**

2. **Hungary / Stipendium Hungaricum.** Barely any Bangladeshi consultancy markets Hungary seriously. EduExpress already lists it — this is a near-empty niche with a fully-funded EU government scholarship. **Big SEO + content opportunity.**

3. **Student-story proof, not stat claims.** EduExpress has student photos and testimonials. Competitors post stock graphics. Real faces of the 2,000+ enrolled = trust no rival can fake.

4. **Founder/consultant credibility in Bangla.** Most pages are English, generic, brochure-style. Short Bangla video explainers (TikTok/Reels) from named consultants are nearly absent at Tier 2. **TikTok is wide open** — almost no serious consultancy is there yet.

5. **Deadline-driven urgency content.** CSC closes ~30 April; GKS, Stipendium Hungaricum, UK intakes all have hard dates. A "deadline tracker" content series creates natural, recurring urgency competitors don't exploit.

---

## 5. Recommended positioning statement

> **EduExpress International — the China, Korea & Europe specialists who show you the real numbers.**
> 8 years, 2,000+ students placed, 98% visa success. We don't just say "scholarships available" — we show you exactly what's funded, what it costs, and which deadline to hit.

## 6. Three things to act on this week
1. Claim the **"real cost & scholarship numbers"** content lane before a rival does (feeds the content engine).
2. Stand up a **Hungary / Stipendium Hungaricum** content + landing push — almost zero competition.
3. Start **Bangla short-form video** on TikTok/Reels with named consultants — the channel rivals are ignoring.
